How it works

Step 1
Create an account & login

Users sign in through the authentication system. Admin accounts are able to manage quizzes and users.

Step 2
Build and assign quizzes

Admins define questions, categories, and difficulty levels, then assign quizzes to learners.

Step 3
Take quizzes & review results

Learners complete quizzes online and can later review scores and feedback. Admins see an overview of activity.
